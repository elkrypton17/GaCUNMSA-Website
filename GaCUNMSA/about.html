Who We Are

Welcome to GaCUNMSA

The Garden City University Nursing and Midwifery Students' Association (GaCUNMSA) is the premier student organization representing nursing and midwifery students at Garden City University, Kumasi.

Established with the vision to nurture competent, compassionate, and ethical healthcare professionals, GaCUNMSA provides a platform for students to develop both academically and professionally while making a positive impact in our communities.

Our Mission

To empower nursing and midwifery students through quality education, professional development, and community service.

Our Vision

To be the leading student nursing and midwifery association in Ghana, producing exceptional healthcare professionals.

Our Principles

Core Values

Compassion

We demonstrate empathy and kindness in all our interactions with patients, colleagues, and the community.

Excellence

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards in nursing and midwifery education and practice.

Service

We are dedicated to serving our communities and improving healthcare outcomes for all.

Empowering future healthcare leaders through excellence in nursing and midwifery education at Garden City University College.
