Admission Requirements

  • WASSCE/SSSCE with credits in 3 core and 3 elective subjects
  • Core Subjects: English, Mathematics, Integrated Science
  • Elective Subjects: Biology, Chemistry, Physics/Elective Mathematics
  • Minimum aggregate score of 24 or better

  • WASSCE/SSSCE with credits in 3 core and 3 elective subjects
